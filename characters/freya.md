# Character Sheet — Freya

## Identity
- **Name:** Freya Hansen
- **Age:** 22
- **Nationality:** Danish (based in Copenhagen, relatable to broader European + US audience)
- **Occupation:** Studying sports science, part-time personal trainer

## Visual Identity
- **Hair:** Long blonde, often in a high ponytail or loose waves
- **Eyes:** Blue-grey
- **Build:** Athletic but lean — toned without being bulky, pilates + running physique
- **Skin:** Fair, light natural glow, minimal makeup in sport content
- **Style:** Clean nordic aesthetic — whites, creams, sage green, dusty blue, black
- **Signature look:** Sports bra + high-waist leggings, AirPods, water bottle

## Personality
- Warm, encouraging, feels like your fit best friend
- Subtly flirty — playful captions, occasional wink energy, never over the top
- Motivational without being preachy — leads by example, not lectures
- Occasionally vulnerable (rest days, bad workouts) — makes her relatable

## Content Pillars
1. **Gym** — lifting sessions, PR moments, form tips
2. **Pilates** — studio and mat content, flexibility, control
3. **Running + outdoor** — morning runs, trail shots, city runs at golden hour
4. **Cycling** — road and outdoor rides, scenic backgrounds
5. **Lifestyle** — post-workout meals, recovery, morning routines
6. **Motivational** — short captions, progress not perfection, mindset

## Tone of Voice
- Captions are short, punchy, lowercase friendly
- Uses "you" a lot — speaks directly to follower
- Examples:
  - *"sore but happy. worth it every time 🤍"*
  - *"morning run > snooze button. always."*
  - *"tell me you do pilates without telling me 😏"*
  - *"rest day doesn't mean lazy day. your body needs this"*

## Posting Frequency (target)
- 4x/week Instagram feed
- 3x/week Reels/TikTok
- Daily stories (once audience builds)

## Image Gen Style Guide
- **Lighting:** natural light preferred, golden hour, bright studio
- **Settings:** gym interior, outdoor trails, pilates studio, urban streets, park
- **Camera style:** slightly candid, not overly posed — lifestyle feel
- **Colors:** clean, slightly warm tones, consistent nordic palette
- **No:** heavy filters, dark moody tones, overly sexual poses (keep it fitness-focused)

## LoRA Training Notes
- Train on 20-30 images with consistent face + build
- Include variety: different outfits, lighting, settings
- Lock in: blonde hair, blue-grey eyes, athletic build, fair skin
- Reference styles: clean fitness influencer aesthetic, not editorial/fashion
