## Content Pillars

### 1. Progress / Results (most important)
Show numbers. Followers, likes, DMs received, money earned.
Nothing performs better than proof.
- "my AI influencer just hit 1000 followers (here's what I did)"
- "first $100 from a fake Instagram girl"
- "30 days in — here's what actually worked"

### 2. Behind the scenes / How it works
Demystify the process. People are curious and slightly shocked.
- "how I generate her photos (full workflow)"
- "the AI tool that makes her look real"
- "how I write her captions without touching them"
- "this is what her DMs look like"

### 3. Fails / Honest moments
Builds trust and makes content relatable. Don't only post wins.
- "I spent 3 hours on this and got 4 likes"
- "Instagram flagged my account — here's what happened"
- "the tool I thought would work that completely flopped"

### 4. Educational / Tips
Positions you as the expert. Seeds the course idea.
- "the 3 tools you need to build an AI influencer"
- "why most AI influencer accounts fail in week 1"
- "how to train a LoRA (explained simply)"
- "the content strategy I'd use if I was starting over"

### 5. Trend-jacking / Hot takes
Piggyback on viral topics to get reach.
- "everyone is building AI influencers now — here's why most will fail"
- "is this ethical? my honest answer"
- "AI influencers vs real influencers — the numbers don't lie"

## Video Format (each video)

```
0:00-0:05  Hook — say the most interesting thing first
0:05-0:15  Setup — 1 sentence of context
0:15-0:50  Meat — show don't tell, screen recordings, results
0:50-0:60  CTA — "follow for part 2" or "comment X if you want the tutorial"
```

- Talk to camera (face-swapped via HeyGen) + screen recordings
- Captions always on (85% of TikTok watched muted)
- Trending audio underneath where possible
- Keep energy up — cut dead air aggressively in CapCut

## HeyGen Setup (one-time)
1. Sign up at heygen.com — Starter plan (~$29/month)
2. Create your avatar: record 2 min of yourself talking to camera (good light, neutral background)
3. HeyGen generates a face-swap avatar from that footage
4. For each video: record yourself → upload → HeyGen outputs face-swapped version
5. Download → edit in CapCut → post
