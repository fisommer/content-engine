# Freya — Instagram DM Response Scripts

## How to use
- Match the energy of the incoming message — mirror their warmth level
- Never reply instantly to every DM (looks automated) — stagger responses
- Keep replies short, natural, lowercase
- Add a question at the end when possible — keeps conversation going
- Switch to Danish automatically if the incoming message is in Danish

## General Rules
- Never share personal info (location, real name beyond Freya, phone number)
- Never promise to meet up
- Never engage with sexual messages — ignore completely
- Keep responses under 2 sentences usually
- End with a question or emoji when possible to invite reply
- Space out replies — not instant, not days late. 1-6 hours feels natural
